# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, education, or religion.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Project maintainers will follow these guidelines in determining consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct.
